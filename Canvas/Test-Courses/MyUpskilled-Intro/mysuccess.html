MySuccess

"My Success" has been designed to help you prepare for vocational educational study. In general, people choose to study online for flexibility and convenience and may not have not studied since high school. You may even have had a negative education experience in the past. "My Success" provides you with information to help build your skills in the areas of question analysis, reading, writing and research skills as well as providing you with tips to successfully progress through your course.

My Upskilled

My Upskilled is your Learning Management System which is also referred to as the LMS. It is the online vehicle through which we deliver your learning and assessment program to you. You can communicate with your fellow students and your trainer here. You have access to your course in the LMS 24/7/365 during your enrolment period.

My Mentor

Your mentor is a Student Support specialist, experienced in dealing with student queries and issues. You will receive a call from your personal Upskilled mentor within 24 - 48hrs of your enrolment into My Success. Your mentor will provide you with your login details and will show you how to navigate through our learning system. Their role is to guide and advise you through your first steps and they will stay with you until you complete your first unit of competency.

My Trainer

You will be allocated a designated trainer for the duration of your course. Your trainer's role is to provide training, mentoring and assessment guidance. Your trainer will be in contact with you within 7 days of your course start date.

My Student Support

For any queries that you may have, you can contact Student Support via our Call Centre - 1300 009 924 or via email studentsupport@upskilled.edu.au. The Student Support Call Centre is available Eastern Standard Time 8:30 am to 5:30 pm Monday - Friday.

My Student Well-being

All too often unfortunately, we hear that students need emotional and well-being support and advice. Please see the workplace mental health website "Heads Up" when you feel you need additional help to get through your day and your studies.
